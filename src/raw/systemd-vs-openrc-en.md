## Systemd vs OpenRC: Which to Choose?

The choice of init system is one of the most debated decisions in the Linux community. Let's analyze the pros and cons of both.

**Systemd** offers an integrated ecosystem with journaling, network management, and centralized logging. **OpenRC** remains the choice for those who prefer simplicity and POSIX compliance.
