FAQ

Is this really free during beta?

Yes. The entire beta period is completely free. No credit card required. No hidden fees. You'll get full access to LogWhispererAI at no cost.

What languages does the AI support?

During beta, AI responses are in English. Italian and other languages are planned for the final release. You can upload logs in any format.

How accurate is the analysis?

LogWhispererAI uses generative AI guided by a curated sysadmin knowledge base and our internal methodology built on 20+ years of Linux/Unix experience. Every recommendation passes through validation rules that flag destructive commands before showing them to you. The AI can still get things wrong — Safety First is what catches it.

What log formats can I upload?

Any text-based log format: syslog, kernel.log, dmesg, journalctl, Apache, Nginx, Docker, Kubernetes, or custom application logs. Max file size: 1MB.

Will this work for production servers?

LogWhispererAI analyzes log files — it doesn't connect to your servers. You can safely upload anonymized logs from any environment including production.

How do I get my free log analysis?

Fill out the signup form below and describe or attach the log that's causing you trouble. We'll analyze it and send the solution to your email within 24 hours.
